Help

The CPT Eligibility tool helps international students quickly determine if they qualify for Curricular Practical Training (CPT). Instead of searching through multiple departments and lengthy pages, students can view clear eligibility requirements, required documents, deadlines, and a step-by-step process all in one place. This streamlined approach reduces uncertainty and allows students like Nadia to confidently confirm their eligibility and take the next steps toward securing their internship without risking delays to their graduation or visa status.

Book Appointment

Students can schedule an appointment with library staff for personalized support with research, assignments, and academic resources. Booking an appointment ensures dedicated time to receive guidance on topics such as finding sources, using databases, or improving study strategies. This option is ideal for students who need more in-depth assistance or prefer structured, one-on-one help.

Walk-in info

Students are welcome to visit the library without an appointment during open hours for studying, research, and accessing available resources. Walk-in access provides flexibility for those who need a quiet space, computer access, or immediate assistance from library staff. During peak hours, seating and computer availability may be limited, so arriving earlier is recommended for the best experience.
